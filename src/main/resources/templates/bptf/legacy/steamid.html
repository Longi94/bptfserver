How to find your steam ID (for use with the bptf mobile app)

1) Go to your steam profile either in your browser or in Steam.
If you use steam make sure the 'Display URL' option is enabled.

1a) Optional. Set a custom profile URL if you haven't already by clicking on Edit on your profile page.

2) Find your ID.
Have a look at the link of your profile in your browser or in Steam at the top of the window. If you have set a custom URL, your profile link will look like this:
http://steamcommunity.com/id/customurl

If you haven't set a custom URL, your profile link will look like this:
http://steamcommunity.com/profiles/7656119XXXXXXXXX

In either case your ID you have to usein the app is the last part of your profile link. So in the above example it would be customurl or 7656119XXXXXXXXX.
Copy your ID into the app and you should be able to login just fine.
